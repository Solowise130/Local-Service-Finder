Our Story

The Local Service Finder is a collaborative Project put up by a team of four vibrant ALX Software Engineering trainees to showcase the skills acquired in our one-year of intensive training in the ALX Cohort9 2022/23 Session.

Our Mission

"Our mission is to empower individuals and communities by connecting them with a diverse range of essential services, fostering collaboration, and breaking down geographical barriers. Through our innovative web application, we strive to simplify access to services, enhance convenience, and promote economic growth for service providers worldwide."

Our Vision

"Our vision is a world where everyone can effortlessly access, share, and benefit from a wide spectrum of services, regardless of their location."
